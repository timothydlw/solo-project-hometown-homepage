Visit Irving

Enjoy culture and sports in this vibrant city in the heart of DFW.

Top three activities to do at Irving

Attend a show or a concert

The Toyota Music Factory is a great place to spend your night out.

Go for a picnic in the local park

Centennial Park is perfect for picnic, getting some a tan, or just chilling out.

Play sports at the Recreation Center

Play volleyball, football, basket ball or lift some weights.

Top three historical sites in Irving

Irving Heritage House

Built in 1912 by C.P. Schulze, this home is a designated Texas State Historical Landmark.

Jackie Townsell Bear Creek Heritage Center

This site explores the history of the Bear Creek community, one of the oldest African American settlements in Dallas County.

Irving Heritage Park

This park features the city's original railroad depot, an authentic 1880s restored log cabin, and the first city library
